    • Lake Mary Hockey League Rules

    Game Length and Time Management

    Two 20 minute halves per game. 5 minutes for half-time. 5 minutes before each game will be allowed for warm-up.

    Clock stops on every goal and penalty. The last 2 minutes of the game, clock will stop for every stoppage.

    The “mercy rule” will apply on a 10 goal lead. After the 10 goal lead, all stats tracking will stop; however, both teams can choose to continue to play.

    Overtime

    Regular Season overtime rules will be 5 minute sudden death. If there is no score within that time period, a shootout will occur.

    Playoff overtime rules will be a 20 minute sudden death overtime period. If there is still no score after the sudden death overtime period, another 20 minute sudden death overtime period will be played and so on until a goal is scored.

    Minimum Players Per Team

    4 players and a goalie for a total of 5.

    Minimum Players Per Game

    3 players and a goalie.

    Equipment Requirements

    1. Helmet
    2. Cage, face shield or visor (optional)
    3. Mouthpiece (optional)
    4. Hockey gloves
    5. Skates
    6. Knee and shin guards
    7. Elbow pads (optional)

    Stats

    For each player the following stats that will be tracked are: Goals, Assists, Points and Penalties.For goalies the following stats that will be tracked are: Goals Against Average and Wins.

    Team Colors

    The home team will wear white and the away team will wear dark.

    Standings

    Standings will consist of Wins (2 points per win), Losses, Points, goals for, and goals against. In the event of a tie at the end of the season Goals for will determine tiebreaker. If goals are tied, Goals against will be used to determine tiebreaker. If Goals against is also tied. Head to head will determine the tiebreaker.

    Playoffs

    Top 4 teams make the playoffs. Playoffs will be one-loss elimination and will be set up as follows: 1st place vs. 4 th place and 2nd place vs. 3rd place. Winners play for championship and losers play for 3rd place.

    Rain-outs

    In the event of a rain out, the game will be rescheduled to a mutually agreed upon time by both teams.

    Trophies

    The following trophies:

    1. Championship trophy
    2. Goals
    3. Assists
    4. MVP
    5. Best Goalie (GAA)
    Games will be on Tuesday and Wednesday nights. Game times will either be at 7:00 p.m. or 8:00 p.m.

    Penalties

    2 minute minor for the following:

    1. Checking
    2. Tripping
    3. High-sticking
    4. Slashing
    5. Holding
    6. Hooking
    7. Roughing
    8. Unsportsmanlike play

    5 minute major for the following:

    1. Spearing
    2. Charging
    3. Boarding
    4. Intent to injure (possible game suspension)

    Fighting

    Any player involved in a fight will be kicked out of the league for an indefinite period of time. No refund will be given to the player.

    All participants must comply with the rules and regulations of this league. Please respect the referees,your teammates and other players. We expect everyone to have fun competitively with respect and sportsmanship. Thank you for your participation and cooperation.
